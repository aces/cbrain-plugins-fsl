Task options for Bedpostx

Based on the FSL documentation at the FMRIB's Diffusion Toolbox

Options:

  • Fibers:Number of fibres modelled per voxel.
  • Weight:Multiplicative factor for the prior on the additional modelled fibres. A smaller factor means more weighting for the additional fibres.
  • Burn in:Number of iterations before starting the sampling. These might be increased if the data are noisy, and the MCMC needs more iterations to converge.
