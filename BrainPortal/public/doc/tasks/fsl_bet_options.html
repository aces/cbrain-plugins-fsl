BET options

Not all options listed are provided on CBRAIN
To request additional options, leave feedback.


This tool will performs BET of FSL.

Options:
  • Fractional intensity threshold (-f): fractional intensity threshold (0->1); default=0.5; smaller values give larger brain outline estimates.
  • Vertical gradient in fractional intensity threshold (-g): vertical gradient in fractional intensity threshold (-1->1); default=0; positive values give larger brain outline at bottom, smaller at top.
